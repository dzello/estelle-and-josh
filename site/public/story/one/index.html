It began in Thailand

Estelle and Josh met in Thailand in the summer of 2015 while volunteering at a place called the Mindfulness Project. People from all over the world come to the project to learn about yoga, meditation, Buddhism and permaculture.

One of their first encounters found them creating mud bricks together, as shown here in the first picture. Long stretches of manual labor, also known as karma yoga, afforded volunteers the opportunity to talk at length and get to know each other.
